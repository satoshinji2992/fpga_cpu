# Tomori Pajama look mechanics

Tomori keeps her bird slippers, feet, and lower torso anchored. Her pink-brown eyes lead, followed by a restrained turn of her short purple-gray bobbed head and a very small upper-body follow-through. The bird-and-bow sleep cap remains attached and follows the head with slight fabric lag; the off-shoulder ruffles, shorts, ankle garters, and slippers keep their shape.

Each 22.5-degree step changes pupils, eyelids, nose direction, head turn, and cap overlap by a similar small amount. Preserve facial proportions and do not rotate or warp the whole sprite.

- 000 up: pupils lift, chin rises slightly, and the cap tips back subtly.
- 090 screen-right: pupils and nose point to the image right; more left cheek is visible and right-side bangs overlap.
- 180 down: pupils lower, chin tucks, and bangs cover slightly more forehead.
- 270 screen-left: pupils and nose point to the image left; more right cheek is visible and left-side bangs overlap.

Diagonals interpolate continuously. No prop, text, shadow, or detached effect is introduced.
